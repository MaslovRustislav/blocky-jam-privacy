Privacy Policy for Blocky Jam

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Introduction

Blocky Jam ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile puzzle game developed as an independent project. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Blocky Jam stores the following data locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers:

  • Game progress (completed levels, star ratings, best move counts, best times)
  • Game settings (sound, music, haptics preferences, game mode)
  • Daily challenge completion and streak data
  • Cosmetic preferences (skins, themes)

We do not have access to this data. If you uninstall the app, this data is deleted.

Data Collected by Third-Party Services

Blocky Jam uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob — We display optional rewarded advertisements through Google AdMob. When you choose to watch an ad, Google may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. Ads are never forced — they are always opt-in (e.g., to earn bonus moves).

Data We Do NOT Collect

Blocky Jam does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Account or login credentials (there are no accounts)
  • Analytics or usage data to our own servers

Children's Privacy

Blocky Jam does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not require any personal information to function. Rewarded ads served by Google AdMob comply with Google's policies regarding child-directed content.

Data Retention

All game data is stored locally on your device. We have no servers and retain no user data. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

Your Choices

  • Ads: You can choose not to watch rewarded ads. They are always optional.
  • Data deletion: Uninstall the app to delete all locally stored data, or use the "Reset Progress" option in Settings.
  • Ad personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's settings (Settings > Google > Ads on Android).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
